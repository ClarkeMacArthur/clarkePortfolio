Who Am I

Hello

Hello! If you've found my page, then you know that I am Clarke, and I am a front end developer (I am also nonbinary - they/them please!). I have a big passion for writing clean, dry, readable and accessible code as well as problem solving and figuring out puzzles.

Having worked in the veterinary industry and having owned my own business, I know that I thrive in teams and busy environments. I enjoy breaking downconcepts for others as well as learning new ways of approaching challenges from my peers.

In my free time, you'll probably find me playing ukulele, learning American Sign Language, playing videogames, or lost in a good book. I am a lifelong learner - I try to learn something new each day from a new song to a new recipe. I think that's why I love coding so much - the learning never stops. It's a puzzle that keeps evolving, and I want to find as many different ways to solve it as I can!
